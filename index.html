About WBS 2026

ORGANIZED BY WORKLINK CONSULTING

A forward-thinking business and development consulting firm committed to connecting ideas, people, and opportunities into impactful results.

WBS 2026 creates a platform where global experts meet African leaders, designers, technologists, city managers, and investors to rethink how branding and signage can reposition Nigeria and Africa on the world stage.

Visually

Transforming Africa's visual identity through innovative branding and signage solutions

Economically

Creating economic opportunities through strategic branding and business development

Culturally

Celebrating and promoting Africa's rich cultural heritage on the global stage

Our Focus

We are focused on improvement and development in Africa, identifying and addressing the loopholes that have hindered progress. WBS 2026 is committed to creating actionable solutions that drive real change across the continent.
